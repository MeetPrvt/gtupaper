How to download papers?

  • First, select SemesterWise or Search Subject.
  • If you select Search Subject, you need to add the subject code or name in the input field.
  • Then subjects will be displayed in both Semesterwise & Search Subject with paper links (for example: Winter - 2022).
  • Now click on the link, and a new window will open with the paper PDF. You can view it or download it.

Why should you prepare from previous year paper's for GTU exams ?

  • Familiarity: Previous year papers give you a sense of the exam pattern, types of questions, and marking scheme, which helps you feel more comfortable during the actual exam.
  • Understanding: Going through old papers helps you understand the depth and breadth of the syllabus, allowing you to prioritize topics based on their frequency of appearance in exams.
  • Practice: Solving previous year papers serves as excellent practice, helping you gauge your preparation level and identify areas where you need to improve.
  • Time Management: By practicing with old papers, you can refine your time management skills, ensuring that you can complete the exam within the allocated time frame.
  • Confidence Boost: Successfully solving previous year papers boosts your confidence and reduces exam anxiety, as you feel more prepared and familiar with what to expect.
  • Strategic Preparation: Analyzing previous year papers can help you identify recurring themes or types of questions, allowing you to tailor your study approach accordingly.
  • Identify Important Topics: Previous year papers often reveal which topics are frequently tested, enabling you to prioritize your revision efforts accordingly.
  • Realistic Simulation: Working through old papers provides a realistic simulation of the actual exam environment, helping you adapt to the format and conditions of the test.
